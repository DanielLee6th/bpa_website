About Business Professionals of America

Business Professionals of America (BPA) is a premier national organization for students preparing for careers in business, office administration, information technology, and other related fields. BPA provides a range of opportunities for personal and professional growth, focusing on leadership, academic achievement, and workplace readiness.

Founded in 1966, BPA now has over 45,000 members across the United States. The organization's core mission is to prepare students to lead, collaborate, and excel in the workplace.

Why Join BPA?

  • Gain invaluable experience in leadership and management.
  • Compete in national and state-level events to demonstrate your skills.
  • Develop your professional network by connecting with industry leaders and peers.
  • Receive hands-on learning opportunities that enhance your future career prospects.
  • Be part of an organization that encourages personal growth and community involvement.

Benefits of Joining BPA

  • Leadership Development: BPA offers students the chance to develop their leadership skills by taking on active roles in their local chapters.
  • Workplace Skills: Through BPA's Workplace Skills Assessment Program (WSAP), students can demonstrate their proficiency in real-world business skills.
  • Competitive Edge: BPA members gain a competitive edge in the workforce, with access to scholarships and other career-boosting opportunities.
  • Networking: BPA provides numerous opportunities to network with professionals and peers through national conferences and industry events.
  • Recognition: BPA members receive recognition for their accomplishments through awards, certificates, and honors.
